# TermsTrap — What are you actually agreeing to?

Paste any Terms of Service and instantly see what you're really signing up for. AI highlights data selling, forced arbitration, auto-renewals, and every clause companies hope you won't read.

![TermsTrap Screenshot](screenshot.png)

## Features

- **Instant ToS Analysis** — Paste any Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, or EULA and get a full breakdown in seconds
- **Visual Risk Score** — A 0-100 risk score with dramatic visual ring showing how dangerous the terms are
- **Trap Detection** — Identifies data selling, forced arbitration, auto-renewals, content rights grabs, liability waivers, and more
- **Plain-English Translations** — Every legalese clause translated into language anyone can understand
- **Grade Breakdown** — Letter grades for Data Privacy, User Rights, Transparency, Fairness, and Accountability
- **Deep-Dive Analysis** — Click any trap to see legal context, real-world cases, opt-out options, and protective actions
- **Good Clause Recognition** — Balanced analysis that also highlights what companies got right
- **Company Comparisons** — See how the terms compare to other major companies
- **Shareable Results** — Download a social-media-optimized PNG card or copy a text summary
- **Sample ToS Included** — Try with built-in fictional ToS examples from SocialBuzz, CloudVault Pro, and QuickLend

## How to Use

1. Open the app in your browser
2. Paste any Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, or EULA into the text area
3. Click "Scan Terms" or press Ctrl+Enter
4. Review the risk score, grade breakdown, and individual traps
5. Click "Learn More" on any trap for deep analysis
6. Share your results with the share button

## Tech Stack

- Vanilla HTML, CSS, JavaScript (no frameworks)
- Runs entirely in the browser
- AI analysis via proxy API
- Google Fonts: Bricolage Grotesque, Outfit, JetBrains Mono
- html2canvas for PNG export
